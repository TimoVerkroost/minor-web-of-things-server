Instructions

  1. Upload the correct code to your box.
  2. Give the box a power supply. (Battery, powerbank)
  3. Press the button, when you are thirsty.
  4. See who's box is vibrating and blinking.
  5. If it is your box, you need to get the drinks (see the color for what drink you need to get).
  6. If it is not your box, that lights up. You can just sit and wait for the coffee, to be brought to you.

Colors

  • #d0f0c0 Light green: When the outside temperture is less than or equal to -5 ℃, the system will chose the color light green, when a user selects, that he/she wants something to drink. This color shows, that the best possible drink for that temperture is a cup of tea.
  • #a46331 Brown: If the outside temperture is between -4 ℃ and 15 ℃, the system will chose the color brown, to show that the best drink is Coffee (and who doesn't like coffee?).
  • #ffff00 Yellow: When the outside temperture is more than 15 ℃, the system will chose the yellow, to show that the best possible solution for that kind of heat, is to have a nice cold Beer

    • *These colors a better visable on the led boxes*

Tilt-switch

The tilt-switch detects the orientation of a led box. If the box stands up right, than the box will be on. When the box is laying flat, box will be on standby and won't receive of send any messages, while it is lanying on it's side.

Vibration motor

The vibration motor sends vibrations, to your box when it received a new message. This is, so that the user does not only get a visual feedback (led-lights), but also a auditory feedback (vibrations).
